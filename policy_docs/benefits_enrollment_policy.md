# Benefits Enrollment Policy

**Document ID:** HR-BEN-006
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026
**Applies To:** All full-time employees

## 1. New Hire Enrollment Window

New full-time employees have 30 calendar days from their first day of employment to enroll in health, dental, and vision benefits. Coverage becomes effective on the first day of the month following enrollment. Employees who do not enroll within 30 days must wait until the next Open Enrollment period, absent a qualifying life event.

## 2. Open Enrollment

Open Enrollment occurs annually each November for coverage effective the following January 1. During Open Enrollment, employees may change plans, add or remove dependents, and adjust elections such as HSA/FSA contributions.

## 3. Qualifying Life Events

Employees may make benefits changes outside of Open Enrollment following a qualifying life event, including marriage, divorce, birth or adoption of a child, or loss of other coverage. Changes must be submitted within 30 days of the qualifying event.

## 4. Plan Options

The company offers three medical plan tiers (High Deductible, PPO Standard, PPO Plus), each with company-subsidized premiums. Dental and vision coverage are offered as separate elections. A Health Savings Account (HSA) is available to employees enrolled in the High Deductible plan, with an annual company contribution of $500 for individual coverage and $1,000 for family coverage.

## 5. 401(k) Retirement Plan

Employees are automatically enrolled in the 401(k) plan at a 3% contribution rate 60 days after hire, unless they opt out. The company matches 100% of the first 3% contributed and 50% of the next 2%, for a maximum company match of 4%. Employees are immediately 100% vested in their own contributions and vest in company matching contributions over 3 years.

## 6. Dependent Verification

Employees adding a spouse or dependent to any benefit plan must submit dependent verification documentation (such as a marriage certificate or birth certificate) within 30 days of enrollment or the qualifying event.
