# Remote Work Policy

**Document ID:** HR-RW-002
**Effective Date:** March 1, 2026
**Applies To:** All employees classified as remote-eligible

## 1. Eligibility

Roles are designated remote-eligible, hybrid, or on-site by the employee's department head in coordination with HR. Remote-eligible employees may work from any location within the country of employment, subject to manager approval and applicable tax/legal restrictions.

## 2. Hybrid Work Expectations

Employees in hybrid roles are expected to be present in their assigned office location a minimum of 2 days per week, as scheduled by their manager. Core in-office days are intended to align with team collaboration needs and are set at the department level.

## 3. Equipment and Reimbursement

The company provides a laptop and standard peripherals to all remote and hybrid employees. Employees working remotely more than 3 days per week are eligible for a one-time $500 home office stipend, submitted through the expense reimbursement process within the employee's first 90 days in a remote-eligible role.

## 4. Working Hours and Availability

Remote employees are expected to be available during their team's designated core hours, generally 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM in the employee's local time zone, unless otherwise agreed with their manager. Employees working across time zones from their team should coordinate a documented availability window with their manager.

## 5. International Remote Work

Employees requesting to work remotely from outside their country of hire must submit a request to HR and Legal at least 30 days in advance. International remote work is granted on a case-by-case basis and is not guaranteed, due to tax, legal, and data security considerations.

## 6. Security Requirements

All remote employees must connect to company systems via the approved VPN and must not access confidential company data on personal, unmanaged devices.
