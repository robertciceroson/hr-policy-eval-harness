# Parental Leave Policy

**Document ID:** HR-PL-005
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026
**Applies To:** All full-time employees who have completed 90 days of employment

## 1. Eligibility

Full-time employees who have completed at least 90 days of continuous employment are eligible for paid parental leave following the birth, adoption, or foster placement of a child. Part-time employees working 20+ hours per week are eligible on a pro-rated basis.

## 2. Duration and Pay

Eligible employees receive 12 weeks of paid parental leave at 100% of base salary. Leave must be taken within 12 months of the qualifying event and may be taken continuously or, with manager approval, in two blocks of no less than 2 weeks each.

## 3. Both Parents Employed by the Company

When both parents are employed by the company, each parent is independently eligible for the full 12 weeks of paid parental leave.

## 4. Coordination with State and Federal Leave

Parental leave under this policy runs concurrently with any leave entitlement under the Family and Medical Leave Act (FMLA) or applicable state family leave laws, where such laws apply.

## 5. Benefits Continuation

Health, dental, and vision benefits continue uninterrupted during paid parental leave, with the employee continuing to pay their normal share of premiums through standard payroll deduction upon return, or via direct billing during leave.

## 6. Requesting Leave

Employees should notify their manager and HR at least 30 days before the anticipated start of leave when foreseeable (such as a planned birth or adoption). For unforeseeable events, employees should notify HR as soon as practicable.
